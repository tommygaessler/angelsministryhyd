OUR STORY

God heard the cry of a child in the wilderness

The messenger of God came to help, to comfort, to give hope, love and solution to all the problems. Even still today God is moving with compassion when He hears the cry of a CHILD in remote parts of the developing world and inner cities of the industrialized world. He is looking for angels to send and help the broken-hearted.

God gave us the burden for the lost ones. Angels Ministry is working to bring awareness about child evangelism and equip Sunday School Teachers and Young people with skills and teaching techniques that fulfils God's desire.

"We are called to be the angels God sends to the children who cry out."

— Angels Ministry Team
